Chorely Support

Less reminding, more responsibility.

Getting started

How do I add a kid?

Open Chorely, tap Settings → Add a kid. Give them a name, pick an avatar, and set a PIN if you want their account locked.

How do I add a chore?

From the parent home screen, tap the + Add chore button. Pick which kid it's for, set the points, and optionally set a due date or make it recurring.

How does my kid sign in?

On the welcome screen, tap your kid's name. If you set a PIN, they'll be asked for it.

The chore loop

How does my kid mark a chore done?

From their Today screen, they tap the chore, snap a photo as proof, then tap I did it! The chore goes to you for review.

How do I approve a chore?

You'll see pending chores at the top of the parent home. Tap a chore to see the photo, then tap Approve to credit the points, or Send back to ask them to try again.

What if I want them to redo it?

Tap Send back, pick a reason or type a quick note, and the chore goes back to your kid's Today screen.

Rewards

How do rewards work?

You set rewards (a new toy, screen time, a sleepover, anything). Each reward has a points target. When your kid hits the target, you'll see a "Mark as given" button to confirm you delivered it.

Where do I add rewards?

From the parent home, tap Rewards → + Add reward.

Subscription

How does the trial work?

Chorely is free to try for 14 days with full access. After that, a subscription is required to keep using it.

How do I cancel?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Open the App Store, tap your profile, tap Subscriptions, and you can cancel Chorely there.

Account

How do I delete my account?

Open Chorely → Settings → scroll to the bottom → Delete account. This permanently removes your family's data.

I forgot my password.

On the login screen, tap Forgot password and follow the email instructions.
