Delete Your Chorely Account

Chorely — by Jonathan Olsen

You can delete your Chorely account at any time. This page explains how and what gets removed.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Chorely on your iPhone or Android device.
  2. Sign in to your account (if you aren't already signed in).
  3. Tap the gear icon in the top right of the parent home screen to open Settings.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen.
  5. Tap Delete account under the "Danger zone" section.
  6. Confirm the action when prompted. Your account and all family data will be deleted.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • Your family name and code
  • All kids in your family (names, avatars, PINs, points, levels, streak data)
  • All chores (active, completed, recurring, history)
  • All proof photos uploaded by you or your kids
  • All rewards (set, earned, given)
  • Push notification tokens for your devices

What is kept

Nothing tied to your identity is retained after deletion. We may keep anonymized, aggregated usage statistics (for example, "X chores were completed across all families this month") that cannot be linked back to you or your family.

If you have a subscription

Deleting your Chorely account does not automatically cancel your subscription. To cancel a paid subscription:

  • iOS: Open the App Store → tap your profile → Subscriptions → Chorely → Cancel.
  • Android: Open the Play Store → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Chorely → Cancel.

Apple and Google control subscription billing, so cancellation must happen through them. We do not have access to charge or refund your card.

Co-parents and shared families

If you joined another parent's family via a family code, deleting your account removes you and any data you added. The family itself, the kids, and chores added by the other parent remain with them. Co-parents have their own separate accounts and must delete those individually.

Can't access the app?

Locked out, lost your password, or otherwise can't open the app to delete your account?

Email jco1695@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign up. We will manually delete your account within 30 days of receiving your request.
